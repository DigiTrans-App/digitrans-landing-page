FAQ

Common soft-launch questions

Is this checkout inside ChatGPT?

No. DigiTrust Evidence Assistant in ChatGPT is a public demo. Pricing, checkout, subscriptions, billing, and paid onboarding happen outside ChatGPT.

What happens after payment?

Stripe provides payment confirmation and DigiTrans follows up for onboarding. You may also complete the intake to provide reviewer-safe context for your workflow.

What am I purchasing?

You are purchasing the plan or scoped service described on this page and at checkout. The public ChatGPT demo is not a paid workspace and does not itself deliver the commercial service.

Do plans replace professional review?

No. DigiTrust supports evidence workflows and assurance outputs. Customers remain responsible for their own legal, security, audit, procurement, certification, and compliance decisions.

How do subscriptions renew?

Monthly and annual subscriptions renew on the interval shown at checkout unless canceled. Review the cancellation and refund terms before purchasing.

Can enterprise buyers use procurement?

Yes. Enterprise buyers should use the intake or contact DigiTrans to discuss custom terms, security review, procurement, invoicing, and onboarding.
