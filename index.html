What DigiTrust does

Turn review evidence into a repeatable proof workflow

DigiTrust organizes the information reviewers need to understand an AI workflow: its purpose, owner, data handling, approved technology path, human review, verification result, and customer-safe output.

1

For AI and product leaders

Give reviewers a defined workflow and evidence trail before asking them to approve a pilot, launch, or customer-facing use case.

2

For security and compliance teams

Review purpose, data handling, approval context, evidence mapping, and open gaps in one structured record.

3

For procurement and customers

Receive a customer-safe assurance summary without exposing raw production data, credentials, or internal implementation artifacts.

How it works

From one AI workflow to one auditable packet

The workflow is deliberately simple enough for a soft launch and structured enough to support deeper enterprise review.

1

Define

Name the workflow, owner, business purpose, intended users, and review context.

2

Protect

Classify sensitive inputs and document how they are minimized, transformed, or excluded.

3

Review

Confirm the approved AI path, guardrails, human review, and unresolved questions.

4

Prove

Generate the Evidence Packet, verification result, and reviewer-safe assurance output.
