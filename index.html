Evidence Packet, explained simply

A packet is the receipt for an AI workflow

An Evidence Packet is a verifiable proof record your reviewers can read. It explains what happened in the AI workflow, what checks passed, what risks were handled, and what can be shared safely.

Definition: it is the auditable record that answers, “Can we trust this AI workflow enough to approve it, launch it, or share it with a customer?”
