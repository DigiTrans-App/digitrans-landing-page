Privacy Policy

This app-specific policy explains what data DigiTrans LLC collects or receives when you use DigiTrust Evidence Assistant for ChatGPT, how that data is used, who may receive it, how long it is retained, and what controls are available to users.

1. Overview

DigiTrust Evidence Assistant for ChatGPT is a public demo app that helps prospects, partners, security reviewers, and procurement teams experience evidence workflows inside ChatGPT.

The app uses synthetic demo evidence only. It does not require authentication, does not connect to paid customer workspaces, and does not access real customer evidence records.

The app provides read-only tools that can list synthetic demo evidence packets, summarize demo evidence, generate customer-safe assurance summaries, list demo buyer-readiness gaps, explain the evidence workflow, and compare synthetic evidence snapshots.

The app does not sell subscriptions, collect payment details, create paid workspaces, modify evidence records, approve records, notify outside parties, or perform billing actions inside ChatGPT.

2. Data we collect or receive

When you use DigiTrust Evidence Assistant in ChatGPT, DigiTrans LLC may receive limited data needed to respond to your request.

Data categoryExamples
Prompt and tool request dataThe request you make in ChatGPT when you ask the app to list, summarize, compare, or explain demo evidence.
Tool input valuesDemo packet IDs such as epkt_demo_vendor_review_001, audience labels such as “security reviewer” or “procurement team,” tone choices such as “concise,” severity filters such as “medium,” and selected synthetic packet IDs for comparison.
Tool output dataSynthetic packet metadata, customer-safe demo summaries, buyer-readiness scores, synthetic readiness gaps, workflow explanations, and comparison results.
Technical metadataTimestamp, request route, response status, service health, error category, and similar operational information.
Browser or network metadataIP address, user agent, and request metadata that may be processed by hosting, security, or infrastructure providers.

The public demo app is not designed to collect raw customer data, credentials, secrets, payment information, regulated data, or production evidence records.

3. Data the app does not intentionally collect

DigiTrust Evidence Assistant is not intended to collect or process:

  • Real customer evidence records
  • Raw customer prompts from a paid workspace
  • Credentials, passwords, API keys, tokens, or secrets
  • Payment card data or billing details
  • Production workspace records
  • Raw regulated data
  • Unredacted confidential, personal, or sensitive customer data
  • Files uploaded by users for evidence processing
  • Data from third-party business systems

Users should not enter secrets, credentials, regulated data, or private customer information into the public demo app.

7. Retention

The public demo app uses synthetic demo evidence and does not create user accounts or persistent paid workspaces.

DigiTrans LLC retains operational logs only as long as reasonably needed for security, debugging, reliability, compliance, and abuse prevention. Typical operational logs may be retained for up to 30 days unless a longer period is required for security investigation, legal compliance, or service integrity.

Synthetic demo evidence bundled with the app may remain available indefinitely because it does not contain real customer data.

If you accidentally submit sensitive information into the public demo app, contact DigiTrans LLC at info@digitranshq.com or through the support URL so we can review available logs and delete or redact retained data where reasonably possible.

9. No payment or subscription processing inside ChatGPT

DigiTrust Evidence Assistant does not process payments, collect payment card details, sell subscriptions, create paid workspaces, or upgrade accounts inside ChatGPT.

Pricing, checkout, subscriptions, paid onboarding, billing support, and customer workspace entitlements are handled outside the ChatGPT app on DigiTrust-owned web properties.

10. Children’s privacy

The public demo app is intended for business and professional use. It is not directed to children.

11. Security

DigiTrans LLC uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect the public demo service. The v1 app is intentionally limited to no-auth, read-only, synthetic demo tools to reduce risk.

No system can guarantee absolute security. Users should not enter secrets, credentials, regulated data, or private customer data into the public demo.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. If we add authentication, paid workspace access, production evidence workflows, or additional data processing, we will update this policy before those changes are used in the public app.
