DigiTrust Evidence Assistant is now available in ChatGPT

The public app gives prospects, partners, security reviewers, and procurement teams a safe way to experience synthetic Evidence Packets and customer-safe assurance workflows inside ChatGPT.

Published: July 10, 2026   |   Media contact: info@digitranshq.com

DigiTrans LLC announced that DigiTrust Evidence Assistant is now available in ChatGPT as a public trust surface for evidence-workflow evaluation. The app is designed to help business and technical reviewers understand how DigiTrust turns AI workflow information into structured, reviewer-safe evidence.

A public demo with clear boundaries

The ChatGPT app uses synthetic demo evidence and read-only tools. It can list synthetic Evidence Packets, summarize reviewer-safe evidence, generate customer-safe assurance language, identify synthetic buyer-readiness gaps, explain the workflow, and compare synthetic readiness snapshots.

Public demo only: The app does not access paid customer workspaces, production evidence records, billing data, credentials, or raw customer data. It does not create, modify, approve, delete, or bill anything inside ChatGPT.

Why this matters

Enterprise AI reviews often depend on screenshots, tickets, policy references, meeting notes, and other evidence spread across multiple systems. DigiTrust is intended to organize that review context into an Evidence Packet: a structured record of the workflow, its purpose, data handling, approved AI path, human review, verification result, and customer-safe output.

Commercialization path

The public ChatGPT app is the evaluation surface. Paid DigiTrust services, onboarding, subscriptions, and enterprise review happen outside ChatGPT through DigiTrans-owned web properties. Organizations can request a walkthrough, discuss an Evidence Packet QuickStart, review published pricing, or begin an enterprise conversation.
