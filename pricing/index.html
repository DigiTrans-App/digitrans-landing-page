FAQ

Common pricing questions

Is this checkout inside ChatGPT?

No. DigiTrust Evidence Assistant in ChatGPT is a public demo app. Stripe checkout happens on DigiTrans-owned web properties outside ChatGPT.

What happens after payment?

You will receive Stripe confirmation and DigiTrans will follow up for onboarding. You can also complete the DigiTrust intake form after purchase.

Do these plans replace legal, security, or compliance review?

No. DigiTrust supports evidence workflows and customer-safe assurance outputs. Customers remain responsible for their own legal, security, audit, and compliance decisions.

Can I use Enterprise procurement?

Yes. Enterprise buyers should contact DigiTrans for custom terms, onboarding, security review, and procurement coordination.
